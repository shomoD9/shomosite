This is the public site of Shomodip De. It is meant to behave less like a portfolio and more like a small, navigable wiki of the ideas I am trying to think through in public, the products I build, and the design notes that explain the system around them.

My research interests sit where productivity, AI, psychology, and epistemology overlap. The public side of that work lives here in two forms: prose notes that try to state the ideas clearly, and product notes that track the software systems through which those ideas become concrete.
