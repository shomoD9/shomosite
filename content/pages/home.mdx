This site sits at the intersection of one research interest and two crafts. The research interest is productivity: how people think, organize, and build with less drag and more coherence. The crafts are prose and product: language that clarifies what matters, and product thinking that makes that clarity usable.

Some of that work appears as essays and videos. Some of it becomes small software, websites, or internal systems. Keeping those outputs together matters because the prose, the products, and the systems are not separate lives. They are different forms taken by the same inquiry.

Private work occasionally grows out of what is public here, but that is downstream of the work itself. The point of the site is to make the practice legible.
