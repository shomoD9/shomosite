Productivity is the research center of the practice. It is where questions about tools, attention, workflow, and operational design get pushed far enough to become useful.

That sometimes means AI in a direct way: custom skills, prompts, automations, or a clearer decision about which tools are worth keeping at all. It can also mean a maintainable website or a small internal system that removes recurring drag from the work.

The standard is practical leverage. Less duplicated effort. Less tool confusion. More calm control over what the work requires every day.
