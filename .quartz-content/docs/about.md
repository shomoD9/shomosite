I am a product manager and systems thinker whose public work revolves around productivity, AI, psychology, and the conditions under which human beings create knowledge that is not merely inherited from biology.

This site takes a wiki form because I want ideas to accumulate as linked notes rather than as isolated publication artifacts. The goal is not volume. It is continuity: prose should connect to products, products should connect back to design notes, and the whole public system should remain legible enough to browse like a living notebook.
