Genes can hand us capacities, dispositions, and physical structure. They cannot hand us Euclidean geometry, liberal institutions, particle physics, or a theory of debugging. Those things exist because culture can store error-correcting processes that are not reducible to instinct.

I use the phrase *memetic knowledge creation* to point at that zone of human life where criticism and explanation become the engine of progress. Products matter to me largely because they are instruments for amplifying that process.
