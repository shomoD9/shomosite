An ontology is not just a taxonomy with better manners. It is an attempt to say what kinds of things a domain contains, how those things relate, and what transformations preserve meaning when information moves across tools.

That matters in enterprise product work because search is not enough when the user is trying to reason about a world, not just retrieve a string. Good ontology work reduces the amount of accidental translation a person has to do in their own head.
